66 Unique Restaurant Logo Ideas

Reading Time: 8 minutes

The logo design forms an important part of the aesthetic of a restaurant. Good food puts people in a good mood but the care given to details when you create your logo should be the same as the one you give when creating your cuisine. Looking for a fantastic logo design for your restaurant, bar or café, pop-up shop or food truck, that will be used on your menu, signage, web page, food delivery app, social media and restaurant reviews sites? Whether you’re branding or rebranding your restaurant, make a lasting impression and earn loyal customers with delicious food and excellent service paired up with a professional logo design! We’ve gathered great examples of restaurant logo designs with kitchen symbols, fish or meat symbols, green or black and white, and regional symbols. Discover 66 stunning restaurant logos below to inspire you and create a tasty branding!
